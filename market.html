Market Opportunity

Meeting the global demand for natural, sustainable ingredients.

Why Natural Taurine?

Taurine is a vital amino acid widely used in pet food, aquaculture, nutraceuticals, and functional foods. Currently, the market is over 90% dominated by synthetic taurine derived from petrochemical processes.

There is a growing consumer shift towards "Clean Label" and natural origin ingredients that synthetic alternatives cannot satisfy.

Our Value Proposition

  • ✓ Natural, marine-origin: Extracted directly from mussel broth, not synthesized in a lab.
  • ✓ Premium Differentiation: Allows brands to claim "Natural Source" and "Sustainably Sourced".
  • ✓ Circular Economy: Produced by upcycling an existing waste stream, reducing environmental impact.
