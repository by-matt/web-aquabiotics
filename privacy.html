Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 14, 2026

1. Introduction

At Aquabiotics Sur, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your technical information. This policy describes how we collect and use technical data to improve our "Blue Circular Economy" platform.

2. Data We Collect

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand how users interact with our website. The information collected includes:

  • Technical identifiers (IP address, anonymized).
  • Geographic data (Country, Region, and Municipality/Commune).
  • Device information (Browser type, operating system).
  • Browsing behavior (Time spent on pages, links clicked).

3. Purpose of Collection

The data is used exclusively for statistical analysis and internal reporting to optimize our technical content, improve site performance, and better understand our global market interest in marine biotechnology.

4. Legal Compliance (Chile)

This policy is designed to comply with Chilean Law No. 19.628 on the Protection of Private Life. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Cookies

Our site uses cookies strictly for the functionality of Google Analytics. You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings if you wish to opt-out of this tracking.

6. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, please contact us through our official Contact Page.
