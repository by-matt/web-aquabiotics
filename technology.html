Technology Platform

Advanced marine biorefinery and selective separation technologies.

Raw Material

Mussel cooking broth from industrial processing plants. This effluent is rich in proteins, peptides, and free amino acids, specifically taurine, which is naturally abundant in marine mollusks.

Main Product

Natural marine-derived taurine. An essential amino acid with potent antioxidant and osmoregulatory properties, critical for feline health and beneficial for aquaculture species.

Biorefinery Process

1

Advanced Pre-conditioning

Multistage filtration and clarification of the broth matrix.

2

Selective Concentration

Membrane filtration to retain macromolecules and concentrate target metabolites.

3

Selective Molecular Purification

Proprietary chromatographic polishing to isolate 99%+ pure taurine.

4

Drying & Stabilization

Final formulation into a stable, dry ingredient.

Platform Potential

  • • Multi-product recovery: Potential to extract additional value from other amino acids and marine peptides.
  • • Functional ingredients: Development of new bioactive compounds for nutraceutical applications via controlled biological functionalization.

Development Stage

Current TRL: 3 (Experimental Proof of Concept)

Laboratory-scale validation completed (TRL 1-2). Moving towards pilot-scale validation to demonstrate industrial feasibility and economic viability.

Quality Standards

High Purity

Selective chromatographic separation ensures a high-concentration natural taurine, free from synthetic residues and contaminants.

Industrial Compliance

Our operations adhere to international Quality Management standards, including GMP and GCLP, ensuring safety for food applications.

Total Traceability

Full control over the raw material source from the pristine waters of Southern Chile's mussel processing plants.
