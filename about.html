About Us

Rooted in the South, innovating for the world.

Our Origins

Aquabiotics Sur was born in the heart of the Los Lagos Region in southern Chile, a territory known for its pristine fjords and thriving aquaculture industry. We recognized a paradox: while the mussel industry is a major economic driver, it also generates significant volumes of liquid waste that are currently discarded.

We saw this not as waste, but as an untapped reservoir of valuable marine biomolecules. Our team of biologists and engineers came together with a single mission: to unlock this potential through biotechnology.

Mission

To revolutionize the recovery of marine ingredients through sustainable biorefinery processes, creating value for the industry and the environment.

Vision

To be the global leader in upcycled marine metabolites, setting the standard for circular economy in the food sector.

Location

Strategic operations in Los Lagos Region, Chile—the hub of the Chilean mussel industry.
